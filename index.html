Almond Flour Banana Bread

Dessert 1 hour

Ingredients:
1/3 c. coconut oil
1/3 c. honey or maple syrup
2 eggs
3 very ripe mashed bananas
1/4 c. nut milk
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
2 c. almond flour
1 c. roughly chopped walnuts
1 tsp coconut sugar (for topping)
1/3 banana (slightly ripe, sliced for top)

Steps:
Preheat oven to 330°F. Whisk coconut oil and maple syrup well, then add eggs. Mix in mashed bananas and milk. Stir in baking soda, vanilla, salt, and cinnamon. Add almond flour and mix with a large spoon. Fold in walnuts. Pour into a lightly greased tray. Layer banana slices on top and sprinkle with coconut sugar. Bake about 1 hour.

Note: Let it cool before slicing — it sets beautifully.

Moroccan Fish

Dinner Traditional

Add to pot:
1 red bell pepper
2 tomatoes
3 dried peppers
5–6 garlic cloves
1/3 medium can tomato sauce
1/4 cup oil
1/2 cup water

Cook on medium-high until soft (about 10 minutes). Add fish.

Sprinkle on fish:
1 teaspoon salt
Handful chopped cilantro
Crushed red peppers

Let boil.

In a cup mix:
2 tsp paprika
1/3 oil
1/4 tsp cumin
Pour over fish. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low-medium for about 30 minutes until the sauce thickens.

Key: Don’t be shy with the oil and salt.

Halva Tahini Cake

Dessert Freeze Overnight

Base:
1 pack petite beurre cookies (crushed)
4 heaping tbsp date spread
1 tsp flaky sea salt
Combine with hands and press into base of a springform pan.

Mousse:
2 pints heavy cream
1 cup tahini
1/2 cup date syrup (adjust sweetness to taste)

Mix tahini and date syrup until smooth. Whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold in tahini mixture until no streaks remain. Pour over crust.

Freeze at least 8 hours (overnight preferred). Top with halva, crushed pistachios, and flaky salt before serving.
